## Introduction

### What is Filmshoppee Car OMS?

Filmshoppee Car OMS (Order Management System) is a comprehensive business management platform designed specifically for automobile accessory businesses. It helps you manage your entire operation from vehicle intake to delivery, including customer management, service tracking, invoicing, and financial reporting.

### Key Benefits

- **Complete Vehicle Lifecycle Management**: Track every vehicle from intake to delivery
- **Real-Time Dashboard**: Monitor business performance with live KPIs and analytics
- **Automated Workflows**: Streamline operations with smart notifications and status tracking
- **Financial Management**: Comprehensive invoicing, payment tracking, and P&L reports
- **Customer CRM**: Track customer communications, follow-ups, and service history
- **Multi-User Support**: Role-based access control for different team members
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Automated notifications for customers and team

### Who Can Use This System?

- **Car Accessory Shops**: Manage vehicle installations and services
- **Auto Service Centers**: Track service orders and customer relationships
- **Franchise Businesses**: Multi-location management with centralized oversight
- **Small to Medium Businesses**: Affordable solution for growing operations

## Super Admin Guide

### What is a Super Admin?

Super Admin is the platform administrator who manages all tenants (customer businesses) on the platform. Super Admins have access to:

- All tenant accounts
- Subscription management
- Platform-wide analytics
- User management across tenants
- System settings

### Creating a Super Admin Account

**Note**: Super Admin accounts are typically created during initial system setup by the platform administrator.

#### Step 1: Access Admin Panel

1. Log in with your Super Admin credentials
2. Navigate to **Admin** section from the main menu
3. You'll see the Admin Dashboard

#### Step 2: Admin Dashboard Overview

The Admin Dashboard provides:

- **Total Tenants**: Number of registered businesses
- **Active Subscriptions**: Currently active tenant subscriptions
- **Pending Approvals**: Tenants awaiting approval
- **Platform Analytics**: Overall platform usage statistics

### Managing Tenants

#### Viewing All Tenants

1. Go to **Admin** → **Tenants**
2. View list of all registered businesses
3. See tenant status (Active, Inactive, Trial, Pending)

#### Tenant Details

Click on any tenant to view:
- Company information
- Subscription status
- Payment history
- User list
- Activity logs

#### Approving New Tenants

1. Go to **Admin** → **Tenants**
2. Find tenants with **"Pending"** status
3. Review their registration details
4. Click **"Approve"** to activate their account
5. Or **"Reject"** if they don't meet requirements

#### Managing Tenant Status

You can:
- **Activate/Deactivate**: Enable or disable tenant access
- **Extend Trial**: Give additional trial period if needed
- **View Payment Proofs**: Check uploaded payment documents
- **Send Welcome Email**: Notify tenant of account activation

### Subscription Management

#### Viewing Subscriptions

1. Go to **Admin** → **Subscriptions**

3. Filter by status (Active, Expired, Pending)

#### Managing Subscription Requests

1. Review subscription plan requests

### User Management

## Tenant Registration & Onboarding

### Step 1: Create Your Account

#### Registration Process

1. **Visit the Landing Page**
   - Click **"Register"** or **"Start Free Trial"** button

2. **Fill Registration Form**
   - **Company Name**: Your business/organization name
   - **Admin Name**: Your full name
   - **Email Address**: Your business email (will be used for login)
   - **Phone Number**: Your contact number
   - **Password**: Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password

3. **Submit Registration**
   - Click **"Get Started Now"** button
   - Wait for account creation confirmation

4. **Account Created Successfully**
   - You'll receive a confirmation message
   - Your account is now in **24-hour free trial** period
   - You'll receive your **Tenant Code** (e.g., FS01, FS02)

### Step 2: Login to Your Account

1. **Go to Login Page**
   - Enter your **Tenant Code** (received during registration)
   - Enter your **Email Address**
   - Enter your **Password**
   - Click **"Login"**

2. **First Login**
   - You'll be redirected to your dashboard
   - Complete your company profile setup

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile

#### Company Information

1. Go to **Settings** → **Company**
2. Fill in your business details:
   - **Company Name**: Your registered business name
   - **Address**: Complete business address
   - **City, State, Pincode**: Location details
   - **Phone**: Business contact number
   - **Email**: Business email address
   - **GST Number**: If applicable
   - **Logo**: Upload your company logo (optional)

#### Payment Setup (Important!)

1. Go to **Settings** → **Payment**
2. Upload **Payment Proof**:
   - Upload screenshot or document of payment
   - Payment amount: ₹12,000/year (₹1,000/month)
   - Payment methods accepted: Bank transfer, UPI, etc.
3. Submit for approval
4. Your account will be activated after approval

**Note**: Your 24-hour trial period allows you to explore all features. To continue after trial, payment proof must be approved.

### Step 4: Initial Setup

#### Create Locations

1. Go to **Settings** → **Company**
2. Add your business locations:
   - **Location Name**: e.g., "Main Workshop", "Branch 1"
   - **Address**: Location address
   - **Phone**: Location contact number

#### Set Up Departments

1. Go to **Settings** → **Management**
2. Add departments:
   - **Department Name**: e.g., "Installation", "Sales", "Service"
   - **Color Code**: Choose a color for visual identification

#### Add Vehicle Types

1. Go to **Settings** → **Company**
2. Add vehicle types you service:
   - Car, SUV, Sedan, Hatchback, etc.

#### Create User Accounts

1. Go to **Settings** → **Management** → **Users**
2. Click **"Add User"**
3. Fill user details:
   - **Name**: User's full name
   - **Email**: User's email address
   - **Phone**: Contact number
   - **Role**: Select appropriate role (see User Roles section)
   - **Department**: Assign to department (optional)
   - **Specialization**: For installers (optional)
4. Click **"Create User"**
5. User will receive login credentials via email

## Complete Workflow Guide

### Overview: Vehicle Lifecycle

The complete workflow from vehicle intake to delivery:

```
Vehicle Intake → Assignment → Installation → Invoice → Payment → Delivery
```

### Step 1: Vehicle Intake (Vehicle Inward)

#### Creating a New Vehicle Entry

1. **Navigate to Vehicle Inward**
   - Click **"Vehicle Inward"** from the main menu
   - Click **"New Vehicle Inward"** button

2. **Fill Customer Information**
   - **Owner Name**: Customer's full name (Required)
   - **Mobile Number**: Customer's phone number (Required)
   - **Email**: Customer's email address (Optional)
   - **Address**: Customer's address (Optional)

3. **Fill Vehicle Details**
   - **Vehicle Number**: Registration number (Required)
   - **Model Name**: Vehicle model (e.g., "Honda City")
   - **Make**: Vehicle manufacturer (auto-filled from model)
   - **Year**: Manufacturing year
   - **Color**: Vehicle color
   - **Vehicle Type**: Select from dropdown (Car, SUV, etc.)
   - **Odometer Reading**: Current odometer reading

4. **Service Information**
   - **Expected Delivery Date**: When customer expects vehicle back
   - **Priority**: Select priority level:
     - **Low**: Standard service
     - **Medium**: Normal priority (default)
     - **High**: Urgent service
     - **Urgent**: Critical/emergency
   - **Manager**: Assign a manager for this vehicle
   - **Location**: Select workshop location
   - **Issues Reported**: Describe customer's reported issues
   - **Remarks**: Any additional notes

5. **Add Products/Accessories**
   - Click **"Add Product"** for each accessory/service
   - Fill for each product:
     - **Product Name**: e.g., "Car Wrap", "PPF Film"
     - **Brand**: Product brand name
     - **Price**: Product/service price
     - **Department**: Assign to department
   - Add multiple products as needed
   - Remove products using trash icon

6. **Submit Vehicle Inward**
   - Review all information
   - Click **"Save Vehicle Inward"**
   - Vehicle entry is created
   - Automatic notifications sent to assigned manager

#### What Happens After Intake?

- Vehicle appears in **Vehicle Management** with status "Pending"
- Assigned manager receives notification
- Vehicle is visible in dashboard
- Short ID is generated for easy reference

### Step 2: Vehicle Assignment & Status Updates

#### Viewing Vehicles

1. **Go to Vehicle Management**
   - Click **"Vehicle Management"** from menu
   - See all vehicles with filters:
     - **Status**: Pending, In Progress, Under Installation, Ready for Delivery, Delivered
     - **Priority**: Low, Medium, High, Urgent
     - **Date Range**: Filter by date
     - **Search**: Search by vehicle number, customer name

#### Updating Vehicle Status

1. **Click on a Vehicle**
   - Opens vehicle details modal
   - View complete information

2. **Update Status**
   - Click **"Update Status"** button
   - Select new status:
     - **Pending**: Just received, not started
     - **In Progress**: Work has begun
     - **Under Installation**: Products being installed
     - **Ready for Delivery**: Work complete, awaiting delivery
     - **Delivered**: Vehicle delivered to customer

3. **Assign Installer**
   - Select installer from dropdown
   - Installer receives notification
   - Installer can view assigned vehicles

4. **Add Comments**
   - Click **"Add Comment"**
   - Type your note
   - Attach files/images if needed
   - Comments are visible to all team members

### Step 3: Service Tracking

#### Service Tracker

1. **Navigate to Trackers**
   - Click **"Trackers"** → **"Service Tracker"**

2. **View Service Checkpoints**
   - See all vehicles with service checkpoints
   - Track progress of each checkpoint:
     - **Checkpoint Name**: e.g., "Product Ordered", "Installation Started"
     - **Status**: Pending, In Progress, Completed
     - **Completed At**: Timestamp when completed
     - **Notes**: Additional information

3. **Update Checkpoints**
   - Click on a checkpoint
   - Update status
   - Add completion notes
   - Mark as completed

#### Call Follow-Up

1. **Go to Call Follow-Up**
   - Click **"Trackers"** → **"Call Follow-Up"**

2. **Schedule Follow-Up Calls**
   - **Subject**: Purpose of call
   - **Customer**: Select customer
   - **Next Call Date**: When to call
   - **Notes**: What to discuss
   - **Status**: Pending, Completed, Cancelled

3. **Track Follow-Ups**
   - View all scheduled calls
   - Mark as completed after calling
   - Add outcome notes

### Step 4: Installation & Product Tracking

#### Installer Dashboard

1. **Installer Login**
   - Installers see simplified dashboard
   - Focus on assigned vehicles only

2. **View Assigned Vehicles**
   - See vehicles assigned to you
   - Filter by status (Pending, In Progress)

3. **Update Installation Status**
   - Click on vehicle card
   - Mark products as completed:
     - Check each product/service
     - System tracks completion
   - Update vehicle status when done

4. **Full-Screen Mode** (For Installers)
   - Press **F11** or click maximize icon
   - Navigate with arrow keys (← →)
   - Press **Escape** to exit

### Step 5: Invoice & Payment

#### Creating Invoices

1. **Go to Accounts**
   - Click **"Accounts"** → **"Invoices"**

2. **View Vehicle Invoices**
   - See all vehicles ready for invoicing
   - Vehicles with status "Ready for Delivery" appear here

3. **Add Invoice Number**
   - Click on vehicle
   - Enter invoice number
   - System links invoice to vehicle

4. **Record Payment**
   - Go to vehicle details
   - Click **"Add Payment"**
   - Enter:
     - **Amount**: Payment amount
     - **Payment Date**: When payment received
     - **Payment Method**: Cash, Bank Transfer, UPI, etc.
     - **Notes**: Additional information

#### Payment Tracking

- View payment history for each vehicle
- See pending amounts
- Track overdue payments
- Generate payment receipts

### Step 6: Delivery

#### Marking Vehicle as Delivered

1. **Update Vehicle Status**
   - Go to **Vehicle Management**
   - Find the vehicle
   - Update status to **"Delivered"**

2. **Delivery Checklist**
   - Verify all work is complete
   - Confirm payment received (if applicable)
   - Check customer satisfaction
   - Update delivery date

3. **Post-Delivery**
   - Vehicle moves to "Delivered" status
   - Customer receives delivery notification
   - Vehicle appears in delivered vehicles list
   - Can be filtered in reports

Different team members have different access levels based on their role.

## Best Practices

### For Business Growth

#### 1. Complete Customer Information

- Always collect complete customer details
- Save customer information for repeat business
- Use customer history for personalized service

#### 2. Set Realistic Delivery Dates

- Consider actual work time required
- Factor in product availability
- Account for potential delays
- Communicate clearly with customers

#### 3. Use Priority Levels Effectively

- **Low**: Standard service, no rush
- **Medium**: Normal priority (default)
- **High**: Important customers or urgent work
- **Urgent**: Emergency or critical situations

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Login Issues

**Problem**: Cannot login
**Solutions:**
- Verify tenant code is correct
- Check email and password
- Ensure account is active
- Contact admin if account is locked

**Problem**: Forgot password
**Solutions:**
1. Click **"Forgot Password"** on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Follow instructions to reset password

#### Vehicle Not Appearing

**Problem**: Vehicle not showing in list
**Solutions:**
- Check filters (status, date range)
- Verify you're looking at correct location
- Ensure vehicle was saved successfully
- Check if you have permission to view

#### Status Not Updating

**Problem**: Cannot update vehicle status
**Solutions:**
- Verify you have permission (check your role)
- Refresh the page
- Try again after a few seconds
- Contact admin if issue persists

#### Notification Issues

**Problem**: Not receiving notifications
**Solutions:**
- Check notification settings
- Verify email/phone is correct
- Check spam folder for emails
- Ensure notifications are enabled

#### Payment Proof Not Approved

**Problem**: Payment proof pending approval
**Solutions:**
- Wait 24-48 hours for review
- Ensure payment proof is clear and readable
- Verify payment amount matches subscription
- Contact support if pending too long

#### User Cannot Access Features

**Problem**: User sees limited features
**Solutions:**
- Check user's role assignment
- Verify role has required permissions
- Ensure subscription is active
- Contact admin to update role
